Features

Connect ECUs over the Internet

Access remote ECUs

  • Build your testbench with ECUs from remote sites.
  • Save on shipping costs and delays.

Test at suppliers

  • Iterate on prototypes while they're still in the hands of your supplier.
  • Detect bugs and vulnerabilities early on.

Share limited ECUs

  • Reduce the number of expensive ECUs needed for development.
  • Get the most out of early prototypes.

Link virtual and physical ECUs

  • Test with both vECUs and hardware ECUs in one setup.
  • Gain more flexibility for integration tests.

Software-Defined Testbench

Dynamic wiring

  • Connect your ECUs without plugging physical wires.
  • Hook ECUs into testbenches at the click of a button.

Reproducible tests

  • Store your wiring in a file rather than a shelf.
  • Recreate the setup from months ago in mere seconds.
